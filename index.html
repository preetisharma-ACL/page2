About Crestoria Estate by L&T Realty

Crestoria Estate in Panvel offers a premium lifestyle where luxury residences, lush green surroundings, and modern amenities come together to create a future-ready address for families and investors. Strategically located in a fast-growing Navi Mumbai hub, it ensures excellent connectivity via Atal Setu, Sion–Panvel Expressway, and proximity to the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport, with seamless access to Kharghar, Belapur, and Vashi.

The project features thoughtfully designed 2, 3, and 4 BHK homes with spacious layouts, low-density planning, and high-rise architecture, complemented by world-class amenities like a clubhouse, swimming pool, gym, landscaped gardens, and retail spaces. Spread across a 35-acre township, it also offers scenic views of the Matheran hills and strong growth potential, making it an ideal choice starting from ₹1.18 Cr* onwards.
